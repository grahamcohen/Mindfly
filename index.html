About Mindfly

Mindfly is an innovative iOS game that uses ARKit face tracking technology to create a truly unique gaming experience. Control a mystical firefly through an enchanted forest using your facial expressions and head movements - no touch controls needed!

Navigate through magical obstacles, collect glowing amber shards, use powerful abilities, and record your gameplay with your face overlay to share your skills with friends.

Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are compatible with Mindfly?

Mindfly requires:

  • iPhone X or later with TrueDepth camera (required for face tracking)
  • iOS 16.0 or later

Compatible models include: iPhone X, XS, XS Max, XR, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max, 12, 12 mini, 12 Pro, 12 Pro Max, 13, 13 mini, 13 Pro, 13 Pro Max, 14, 14 Plus, 14 Pro, 14 Pro Max, 15, 15 Plus, 15 Pro, 15 Pro Max, and all newer models.

Is my face data safe? What data does Mindfly collect?

Your privacy is our top priority.

Mindfly processes face tracking data 100% on your device. Your facial data:

  • Is never stored anywhere
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Only exists temporarily in device memory during gameplay

The app has no backend server and all game data (scores, purchases, settings) is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've purchased in-app items (amber shards, glow dust, permanent boosts, or ad removal) and need to restore them:

  1. Open the Mindfly app
  2. Go to the Shop/Store section
  3. Look for the "Restore Purchases" button
  4. Tap it and wait for confirmation

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchases.

What are Amber Shards and how do they work?

Amber Shards are the premium currency in Mindfly. You can:

  • Earn them by playing and achieving high scores
  • Purchase them through in-app purchases
  • Use them to buy power-ups, glow dust, and permanent upgrades
  • Unlock special abilities and boosts

Your amber shards are automatically saved to your device and persist between sessions.

How does the gameplay recording feature work?

Mindfly includes a ReplayKit-powered recording feature that lets you capture your gameplay with your face overlay:

  • Tap the record button before starting a game session
  • Your face will appear in a small overlay while you play
  • The recording captures both your gameplay and your facial expressions
  • Videos are saved to your Photos library
  • Share your best runs with friends!

Note: Face overlay recordings stay on your device and are only saved if you choose to save them.

The game isn't responding to my face movements. What should I do?

If tracking feels off during a game, use the built-in Recalibrate option:

  1. Tap the pause button during gameplay
  2. Tap "Recalibrate" from the pause menu
  3. Look directly at the front camera â€” a progress bar will fill as your face is detected
  4. Once complete, tap "Resume Game" to continue your run

This resets the tracking anchors without ending your game. If tracking still feels off after recalibrating, try these additional steps:

  • Make sure you've granted Camera permission to Mindfly in Settings > Privacy > Camera
  • Ensure your face is well-lit and clearly visible to the front camera
  • Remove any obstructions (sunglasses, masks, etc.)
  • Restart the app
  • Restart your iPhone if the issue persists

If problems continue, please contact support.

How do I remove ads?

Mindfly uses Google AdMob to show ads. You can remove all ads permanently by:

  • Opening the in-app Shop/Store
  • Finding the "Remove Ads" purchase option
  • Completing the one-time purchase

This is a permanent purchase that removes all ads from the app forever, even if you reinstall.
