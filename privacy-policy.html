Mindfly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

Mindfly ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed by Graham Cohen. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

Mindfly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share any data with third parties for marketing purposes

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (high scores, unlocked items, tutorial completion)
  • Game settings (sensitivity preferences, display options)
  • In-app purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Camera and Microphone Usage

Mindfly uses:

  • Camera: For face tracking to control gameplay. The camera feed is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted unless you explicitly choose to record a gameplay video.
  • Microphone: Only used when you choose to record gameplay videos with audio. Audio is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us.

TrueDepth API and Face Data

Mindfly uses Apple's TrueDepth camera and ARKit framework for gameplay and optional personalization features. Here is exactly what is collected and how it is used:

Real-Time Gameplay Tracking

During gameplay, the app reads head orientation (yaw and pitch) and two facial expression values (jaw open and cheek puff) to move the player character and trigger in-game abilities. This numerical tracking data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device within Apple's ARKit framework
  • Held in temporary memory only during an active game session
  • Never written to disk or stored persistently
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Never used for facial recognition, user identification, or authentication
  • Immediately discarded when the gameplay session ends

Optional Gameplay Recording

If you choose to record gameplay with the face overlay feature enabled, camera frames from the TrueDepth session are temporarily stored in device memory and composited into a video file saved locally on your device. This video is never uploaded or shared automatically — it is only shared if you explicitly choose to do so using the iOS share sheet. You may disable the face overlay at any time by toggling the face icon in the recording controls, which results in screen-only recording where your face does not appear in the saved video.

Optional Character Personalization

You may optionally capture a photo of your face from the TrueDepth camera session to use as a custom face on your in-game character. This photo is processed on-device (background removal) and saved locally in the app's storage on your device. Saved face photos are never transmitted to any server or third party. You can view and permanently delete any saved face at any time from the Saved Faces gallery within the app.

No facial maps, meshes, or biometric identification data are collected or retained at any time by any feature of the app.

Apple In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Mindfly does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The game is suitable for all ages.
